Thanks be to God for Robin Martens!

Thanks be to God for Robin Martens! We welcome Robin as our new Director of Music Ministry! Robin will lead worship this Sunday, November 29.

We are excited and blessed to announce the Council approved the hiring of Robin Martens at their meeting last week, Robin graciously and joyously was honored to accept Lord of Life offering her the position! Thank you to all who shared the joy of this announcement on Sunday! We look forward to having Robin lead worship beginning this Sunday, and to our ministry more officially and formally welcoming Robin in the coming weeks!
